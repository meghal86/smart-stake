## Full Description (4000 chars max)
AlphaWhale is your all-in-one DeFi intelligence platform — built for crypto users who want to stay ahead without drowning in noise.

🐋 WHALE INTELLIGENCE
Track what the biggest wallets in crypto are doing in real time. See accumulation patterns, large transfers, and wallet movements across Ethereum, Base, Polygon, and more. Get notified when whales make significant moves.

🛡️ GUARDIAN — PROTECT YOUR WALLET
Your DeFi security co-pilot. Guardian scans your connected wallets for dangerous token approvals — permissions you may have forgotten about that could drain your funds. Revoke risky approvals in one tap before they get exploited.

🎯 HUNTER — CATCH EVERY AIRDROP
Never miss an airdrop again. Hunter tracks upcoming opportunities across 20+ protocols and shows you exactly what actions to take to qualify — before the snapshot deadline.

📊 PORTFOLIO DASHBOARD
Connect multiple wallets and see your complete DeFi portfolio across all chains. Track balances, open positions, P&L, yield, and your overall risk score — all in one clean view.

🔍 RISK SCANNER
Get an instant risk assessment for any wallet address. Identify connections to mixers, sanctioned addresses, and known exploits.

📡 LIVE SIGNALS
Real-time feed of significant on-chain events. Filter by chain, asset size, and signal type. Set custom alerts for the patterns that matter to you.

KEY FEATURES:
• Multi-wallet support across Ethereum, Base, Polygon, Arbitrum & more
• Real-time whale movement tracking and alerts
• One-tap token approval revocation with Guardian
• Airdrop opportunity calendar with qualification tracking
• Portfolio stress testing and scenario modeling
• Dark and light mode
• Works read-only — your seed phrase is never required

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
AlphaWhale connects to your wallet using read-only RPC connections. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrase. Guardian's approval revocation requires your signature through your own wallet — we never sign anything on your behalf.

DISCLAIMER:
AlphaWhale provides on-chain data and analytics for informational purposes only. Nothing in this app constitutes financial advice. Cryptocurrency investments carry significant risk. Always do your own research.
